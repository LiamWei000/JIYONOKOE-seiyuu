About Us

JIYONOKOE Seiyuu is a WhatsApp-based community focused on Voice Club Japan (VCJ) and voice acting (VA).

We bring together anime fans and aspiring voice actors to collaborate, practice dubbing, and explore the art of Japanese voice acting.
